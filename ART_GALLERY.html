Steps to Create Your Own Art

  • Choose your inspiration
  • Gather materials
  • Sketch your idea
  • Apply colors and techniques
  • Let it dry and display

Materials & Techniques Used

  • Acrylic paints
  • Canvas or paper
  • Brushes of various sizes
  • Palette knives
  • Blending and layering
  • Texturing

Gallery Rules

  • Respect the artwork and artists
  • No food or drinks near the displays
  • Photography allowed without flash
  • Keep noise to a minimum
  • Ask before touching any exhibits
